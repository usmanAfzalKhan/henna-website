Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my bridal mehndi appointment?

It is recommended to book your bridal mehndi session at least 2 to 3 months in advance, especially during peak wedding season. Booking early secures your date and allows time for personalized design discussions.

What kind of henna do you use? Is it safe for all skin types?

We use fresh, organic, and chemical-free henna made from natural ingredients. It's generally safe for all skin types.

How long does the mehndi application take for bridal and non-bridal events?

Bridal mehndi can take 3 to 6 hours depending on intricacy. Non-bridal designs usually take 10–20 minutes per person.

How can I make my henna color darker and last longer?

Keep the paste on for at least 6 hours, avoid water for 12 hours after removal, and apply a lemon-sugar sealant while drying.

Do you travel for events? What areas do you cover?

Yes. We primarily serve the GTA but are available to travel with advance notice. Travel charges may apply.

Can I choose a custom design, or do you have pre-set styles?

We offer both! Choose from our portfolio or work with Simra to create a custom design with personal elements.

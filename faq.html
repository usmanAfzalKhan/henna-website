Frequently Asked Questions

How far in advance should I book my bridal mehndi appointment?

It is recommended to book your bridal mehndi session at least 2 to 3 months in advance, especially during peak wedding season. Booking early secures your date and allows time for personalized design discussions, ensuring everything aligns with your outfit, theme, and preferences.

What kind of henna do you use? Is it safe for all skin types?

We use fresh, organic, and chemical-free henna made from natural ingredients. It's generally safe for all skin types, including sensitive skin. However, if you have specific allergies or skin conditions, we recommend a patch test at least 48 hours before the full application.

How long does the mehndi application take for bridal and non-bridal events?

Bridal mehndi can take 3 to 6 hours depending on intricacy and hand/foot coverage. For non-bridal events like parties or festivals, each person’s design usually takes 10-20 minutes. We plan timings based on group size and ensure efficient, high-quality service for all guests.

How can I make my henna color darker and last longer?

To achieve a rich, dark stain, keep the paste on for at least 6 hours or overnight. After removal, avoid water contact for 12 hours. Apply a lemon-sugar sealant while drying and keep the area warm. Refrain from using exfoliating soaps or lotions for long-lasting color.

Do you travel for events? What areas do you cover?

Yes, we do travel for mehndi events. We primarily serve the Greater Toronto Area but are available for travel outside the region with advance notice. Travel charges may apply depending on the distance and duration of the event. Contact us for custom travel arrangements.

Can I choose a custom design, or do you have pre-set styles?

We offer both options. You can select from our existing design portfolio or collaborate with Simra to create a custom design based on your inspiration, culture, or theme. We love incorporating personal elements like initials, symbols, or motifs that make your design unique.
